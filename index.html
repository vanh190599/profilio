About Us

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Van Anh, I'm a full-stack web developer and digital artist.

I have a strong passion for both technology and art, and I always strive to learn new skills to become a leader in the near future.

With over 2 years of experience in web development, I have knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, and some frameworks such as VueJS and Laravel. I always aim to provide the best value for my clients by utilizing the latest techniques and technologies.

In addition to web development, I am also passionate about creating digital art. I have created various pieces using Photoshop and Illustrator, ranging from simple to complex artworks. I always look forward to having more time to improve my drawing skills and create even more beautiful pieces.

I believe that I will become a leader in the near future. I always try to learn from those who are better than me, seek opportunities to participate in large projects, and contribute my ideas to help the project achieve the best success.

Finally, apart from programming and drawing, I also enjoy traveling. I always look forward to having time to explore new places, experience different cultures, and seek new inspirations for my work and life.

Thank you for reading my introduction!
